What is SendLog?

SendLog is an intuitive iOS app designed specifically for rock climbers to easily log, track, and analyze their climbing activities. Quickly record your climbing achievements, access detailed statistics, and keep personalized notes for each climb.

App Features

  • Log details for each climbing route: route name, area, date, grade, rating, send type, and notes.
  • Browse and filter your climbing logs easily.
  • Voice notes feature with multilingual speech recognition.
  • Export and import your climbing logs as CSV files.
  • Detailed statistics to track your climbing performance.
  • Intuitive, climber-friendly design.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, all your data is stored securely on your device and is never shared unless explicitly exported or shared by you.

Q: Can I change the grade system?

A: Yes, you can select from French, UIAA, or YDS in the app settings.

Q: How do I backup my data?

A: You can export your logs from Settings → Export SendLogs.

Q: Can I import my logs from another device?

A: Yes, you can import data from a previously exported ZIP file through Settings → Import SendLogs.

Q: Is voice note recording available in multiple languages?

A: Yes, the app supports voice notes in English, German, French, and Spanish.

Q: Does SendLog require an internet connection?

A: No, SendLog works fully offline, ensuring your climbing data is always available.
