SendLog – Your Climbing Companion

What is SendLog?

SendLog is a simple, voice-powered app for rock climbers to record and remember their sends. Whether you’re at the crag or in the gym, just speak your notes and SendLog will take care of the rest. Over time, build a personal climbing log and gain insights through built-in statistics.

Key Features

  • Voice Logging: Quickly record route notes using speech recognition.
  • Route Details: Save route name, area, grade, personal comments, and star ratings.
  • Organized Logbook: View and filter past sends by area to keep things tidy.
  • Statistics: Track your climbing progress with stats like most climbed areas, average grades, and more.
  • Export & Share: Export logs as CSV, or share your favorite sends as custom images.
  • Customizable Settings: Choose your preferred speech recognition language and grade system.

FAQ

Is SendLog free to use?

Yes, SendLog is completely free to download and use.

Does SendLog work offline?

You can view and manage your logs offline. Voice recognition requires an internet connection.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export all your send logs as a CSV file from the app’s Settings page.
