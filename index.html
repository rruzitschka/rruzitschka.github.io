What is SendLog?

SendLog is an intuitive iOS app designed specifically for rock climbers to easily log, track, and analyze their climbing activities. Quickly record your climbing achievements, access detailed statistics, and keep personalized notes for each climb. And even better, it is free!

App Features

Log Climbing Routes

Record detailed information for each climb including route name, climbing area, date, difficulty grade, personal rating (1-5 stars), send type (redpoint, pinkpoint, on sight), and personal notes.

Project Tracking

Dedicated section to manage climbing projects, track attempts, record high points, add specific notes, update status (active, completed, abandoned), and convert projects into completed sends.

Voice Notes

Conveniently record and transcribe climbing notes using multilingual speech recognition in English, German, French, and Spanish.

Enhanced Sharing

Generate visually appealing climbing achievement cards with send type icons and improved layouts for easy sharing on social media.

Comprehensive Statistics

Gain insights through detailed climbing statistics including total routes climbed, average rating, top climbing areas, and visual grade distribution charts.

Data Import & Export

Securely backup your logs by exporting data in CSV format within a ZIP archive. Import climbing logs effortlessly, including support for older file formats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, your data is stored locally and securely on your device. It is only shared externally when explicitly exported or shared by you.

Q: Can I track climbing projects separately from completed routes?

A: Absolutely. SendLog has a dedicated Projects tab to clearly manage and track all your active, completed, or abandoned climbing projects.

Q: How do I backup and restore data?

A: You can quickly export your logs as a ZIP file for backup and later restore this data using the import functionality, which supports both new and older file formats.

Q: Can I use voice notes in multiple languages?

A: Yes, voice notes can be recorded and transcribed in English, German, French, and Spanish to accommodate diverse users.

Q: Does SendLog require internet connectivity?

A: No, SendLog functions entirely offline, ensuring you always have access to your climbing logs and statistics even in remote locations.

Q: Is historical data from older versions supported?

A: Yes, SendLog fully supports importing data from previous versions, ensuring continuity and preservation of your climbing history.

Q: How is the top climbing grade determined?

A: The app automatically calculates and displays your highest climbing grade based on successfully completed (sent) routes, providing a clear view of your climbing progression over time.
